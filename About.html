About This Site

        The Children's Movies website was developed for individuals like myself, who need a centralized source of appropriate movies for children.  I feel as though today's society has allowed more and more adult content to work its way into our children's viewing material.  The goal of this website is to allow you the user, to have a source of age appropriate movies that you can fell comfortable allowing your young children to watch.  In doing this, you will feel at ease knowing that the Children's Movies website has your children's best interests at heart.

        The following is a list of attributes this site has to offer:

  • Well-known movies made by well-known companies
  • Only movies with a rating of either G or PG
  • Detailed information about each movie
  • The ability to watch the trailer for each movie
