31 January 2012

New on Disney Blu-Ray, DVD & Combo Pack

Just released today … if you haven't already hosted a a four-legged Buddies' marathon, there couldn't be a better time. Grab some popcorn and check out "Snow Buddies" and "Treasure Buddies," two of the most popular films from the Buddies' franchise -- both available now on Disney Blu-ray, DVD, and Combo Pack.

"Snow Buddies"
Epic adventure continues when the puppies head to the Great White North to compete in a dogsled race that challenges them to find their way home.

"Treasure Buddies"
Rosebud, B-Dawg, Budderball, and the gang set out on a search for the greatest treasure known to animal-kind in an adventure that takes them to the ruins of ancient Egypt.

16 November 2011

"Brave" Trailer Debut!

Don't miss the trailer debut of Disney-Pixar's "Brave" TODAY, now playing on Disney.com!

"Brave" is the adventure of an impetuous princess named Merida who's determined to confront tradition, destiny, and the perilous beast who threatens her beloved Scottish kingdom.

Directed by Mark Andrews and Brenda Chapman (both previously worked on numerous Pixar films in various capacities), "Brave" opens in theaters June 22, 2012, and stars the voice talents of Kelly Macdonald, Emma Thompson, Billy Connolly, Julie Walters, Kevin McKidd, Craig Ferguson, and Robbie Coltrane.
